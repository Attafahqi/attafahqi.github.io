Skylite Musicals 2021

Head of Multimedia

Feb 2021 - Nov 2021

Skylite Musicals is a captivating drama performance infused with music and dance, produced by the talented students of SMA Labschool Kebayoran. Traditionally, this theatrical extravaganza was held offline in a theater setting. However, due to the challenges posed by the Covid-19 pandemic, the event was creatively adapted to an online format, where it was recorded and subsequently uploaded to a streaming website. As the Head of Multimedia for this extraordinary production, my role encompassed a range of responsibilities throughout the process. In the pre-production phase, I diligently prepared all recording equipment, ensured the presence of skilled personnel, and handled various logistical aspects. During production, I supervised the actual performance takes, ensuring that everything ran smoothly. Lastly, in the post-production phase, I meticulously selected the best shots and conducted seamless editing, resulting in a polished and captivating presentation. Despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, Skylite Musicals proved to be a resounding success, showcasing the remarkable talent and creativity of SMA Labschool Kebayoran's students to a broader online audience.

Bedah Kampus Universitas Indonesia 22

Documentation and Multimedia Staff

Jul 2022 - Jan 2023

Bedah Kampus Universitas Indonesia is an event designed to introduce the campus of Universitas Indonesia to all prospective new students. This program is organized by the Student Executive Board (Badan Eksekutif Mahasiswa - BEM) of Universitas Indonesia. As a staff member for documentation and multimedia, my responsibilities involve editing video teasers, trailers, and after-movies related to the event. Additionally, I am tasked with documenting the entire event as it unfolds. Throughout the event, I capture and record various aspects of BKUI, ensuring that important moments are preserved for promotional and archival purposes. This documentation includes interviews with participants, speakers, and organizers, as well as covering the different activities and highlights that take place during the event. By creating video teasers and trailers, I aim to generate excitement and interest among potential new students, showcasing the unique aspects of Universitas Indonesia and what it has to offer. Once the event concludes, I will compile all the footage into an after-movie, which serves as a recap of BKUI22, allowing participants and attendees to relive the memorable moments of the event.

University of Indonesia Racing Team

Electrical Engineering Intern

Nov 2022 - Dec 2022

The University of Indonesia Racing Team is a student-led initiative focused on building a Formula Student team and designing a Formula-style racing car from the ground up. During my one-month internship in the electrical division, I had the opportunity to delve into the intricacies of the car's electrical systems. My tasks included studying and understanding the electrical components, hands-on assembly of electrical parts, conducting thorough checks to ensure proper functionality, and taking on an individual project to design and implement a LED brake light for the formula car. Additionally, as a part of the final project, I collaborated with a team to create a comprehensive electrical schematic for the entire formula car using the KiCad software. This internship provided invaluable practical experience, enriching my knowledge of electrical systems in high-performance vehicles and exposing me to the exciting world of automotive engineering and formula racing.

PSB Ganjil DTE UI 2023

Head of Documentation

Dec 2022 - Mar 2023

The PSB Ganjil DTE UI 2023 is a special event organized by the students of the Electrical Engineering Department at the University of Indonesia. Its main objective is to provide a platform for the department's community to show their appreciation to the seniors who have recently graduated during the fall semester of 2023. Our documentation team plays a crucial role in capturing and preserving the important moments of the event. One of our key tasks is to document the newly graduated seniors right after their thesis defense, capturing their joy and sense of accomplishment. These moments will be shared and celebrated through social media platforms as a form of appreciation. Additionally, we are in charge of setting up a photobooth during the main event. This photobooth serves as a fun and interactive area for participants to take memorable pictures and create lasting mementos of the occasion. As the Head of Documentation, I lead and coordinate the team's efforts to ensure that every important aspect of the event is well-documented and preserved. Our aim is to create a comprehensive record of the PSB Ganjil DTE UI 2023, reflecting the appreciation, camaraderie, and memorable experiences shared by all participants.
