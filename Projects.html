Line Follower Robot

Dec 2022 - Universitas Indonesia Robitics Team Recruitment

For Universitas Indonesia Robitics Team Recruitment, We are divided into teams to make Line Follower Robot, after that we divided again into 3 divisions: Mechanical, Electrical, and Programming. In this project, I work in Electrical Division, but because I have experience in making this robot, my team make me as the Team Leader. My job in Electrical Division is making the Electrical Schematic Design, assembling and wiring the electrical part, and making sure the electrical system in this robot works. As the Team Leader, my job is helping Mechanical and Programming division to work on the right path, making team meeting and deadline for each divisions, leading the testing, and making sure the robot works as the way we planned at the start of this project.

Race Timing Device

June 2023 - Basic Digital System Final Project

For our basic digital system final project, our team of four members has decided to create a race timing device based on logical circuits. The idea for this device came from my personal interest in racing sports. The main function of this device is to accurately measure race times from the start until the finish line, including lap times. It is capable of measuring time up to hundredths of a second. To visualize the time, we will use a 7-segment display. As the originator of the idea, I will take on the responsibility of creating the schematic design using the Proteus software. This software will allow us to design, simulate, and test the logical circuits for our race timing device. With a focus on precision and efficiency, we aim to create a reliable and accurate race timing solution for our final project.

Automatic Lamp

June 2023 - Commision Project

After successfully completing the Race Timing Device project, I received an order from a client to create a schematic design for their logical circuit device. The client's requirement is to design a device that can automatically turn on and off a lamp based on motion detection. However, they have an additional feature request: the device should allow users to customize the delay time for automatically turning off the lamp after a person exits the room. To facilitate this customization, a 7-segment display will be used to show the selected delay time and the countdown until the lamp turns off. Furthermore, the client also desires a user-friendly feature: the ability to change the LED color with a single click. This color-changing functionality will provide a convenient way for users to personalize the device to their preferences.
